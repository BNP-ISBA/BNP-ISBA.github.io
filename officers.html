The BNP-ISBA board members

Igor Prünster. Section Chair 2019.

Igor is a Professor in Statistics at the Department of Decision Sciences, Bocconi University. For more information, visit his website.

Fernando Quintana. Section Chair-Elect 2019.

Fernando is a Professor in Statistics at the Department of Statistics, Pontifical Catholic University of Chile. For more information, visit his website.

Peter Müller. Past Chair 2019.

Peter is a Professor of Mathematics at the Department of Mathematics, University of Texas at Austin. For more information, visit his website.

Maria de Iorio. Program Chair 2018-2019.

Maria is a Professor of Biostatistics with joint appointments at University College London and Yale-NUS College. For more information, visit her website.

Mingyuan Zhou. Treasurer 2018-2019.

Mingyuan is an Associate Professor of Statistics at the McCombs School of Business, University of Texas at Austin. For more information, visit his website.

Sara Wade. Secretary 2019-2020.

Sara is a Lecturer (Assistant Professor) in Statistics and Data Science at the School of Mathematics, University of Edinburgh. Her research spans Bayesian nonparametrics and machine learning, with an emphasis on clustering and mixture models, density regression, Gaussian processes, and exact and approximate inference techniques. Applications of interest include, but are not limited to, biomedical studies, particularly in dementia and Alzheimer's disease. For more information, visit her website.
