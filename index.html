BNP-ISBA. The Bayesian Nonparametric Section of the International Society for Bayesian Analysis.

BNP-ISBA is a section of the International Society for Bayesian Analysis (ISBA) aiming to promote the development and application of Bayesian nonparametric methods and inference techniques.

To this aim, we organize the biennial International Conference on Bayesian Nonparametrics and, in alternate years, the biennial Bayesian Nonparametric Networking Workshop. Additionally, we hold other occasional workshops and sessions at other major conferences, along with a dedicated regualar webinar series.

Join BNP-ISBA

Are you interested in Bayesian nonparametrics? Would you like to participate in activities to promote your research and learn about the latest research in Bayesian nonparametrics? Join BNP-ISBA!

We welcome researchers at any level. To become a BNP-ISBA member, subscribe to ISBA here. In the memberships form you will have the possibility to add your one-year membership to BNP-ISBA (Bayesian Nonparametric Section) for only $5 more, or even a lifetime membership for $75.

To stay up-to-date, sign up to our BNP-ISBA google group. Please include your Name and Affiliation when requesting to join.

Events

We organize the biennial International Conference on Bayesian Nonparametrics, to showcase the latest research and foster collaborations. With thirteenth successful previous editions of the conference, we are excited to see our membership and interest in the field grow. Indeed, we are one of the largest section of ISBA. We are also excited to announce the BNP networking workshops, now in its fourth edition. This recently launched series of smaller, flexible workshops is designed to to a) create an informal BNP event characterized by a flexible schedule and slots dedicated to early career researchers, b) establish and strengthen local networks and broaden appeal, c) foster brainstorming and innovation.
