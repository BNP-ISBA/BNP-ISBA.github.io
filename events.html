BNP-ISBA endorses an array of meetings and events around the world.

BNP-ISBA organizes the biennial Internaional Conference on Bayesian nonparametrics, sessions at other meetings (e.g. the ISBA World Meeting), and endorses events, small meetings, or workshops (e.g. at NeurIPS).

Endorsed meetings: BNP-ISBA supports and endorses other meetings, sessions, workshops to encourage activity in Bayesian nonparametrics. To request endorsement, please submit a single PDF file to bnp.isba.section@gmail.com, containing information on the event including Title, Dates, Location, Website (if available), Description, and BNP-ISBA representation (any involvement of BNP-ISBA members).
