The section has been organizing a regular webinar series since September 2022 to share research progress made by distinguished scholars in our field. Although initially motivated by restrictions related to global health conditions, our aim is to establish this series as a permanent platform for regular meetings and discussions on BNP ideas.

Starting in Fall 2024, webinars will be held approximately every two months, typically on the first Wednesday of the month. Information on past and upcoming webinars can be found below.
