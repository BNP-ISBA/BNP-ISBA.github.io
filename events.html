BNP-ISBA endorses an array of meetings and events around the world.

BNP-ISBA organizes the biennial World Meeting on Bayesian nonparametrics, sessions at other meetings (e.g. the ISBA World Meeting), and endorses events, small meetings, or workshops (e.g. at NeurIPS).

We are also excited to annouce the launch of the BNP networking workshops, a new series of smaller workshops that aim to a) create an informal BNP event characterized by a flexible schedule and slots dedicated to early career researchers, b) establish and strengthen local networks and broaden appeal, c) foster brainstorming and innovation. The first two editions will be held in Cyprus, April 2022 and in Melbourne, Australia, November 2022.

The call for proposals for future editions of the biennial World Meeting on Bayesian Nonparametrics usually opens every two years at the event. All future editions are expected to adhere to the clauses for the biennial World Meeting. Proposals for the BNP networking workshop series are open year-round, with an official announcement made every two years. These workshops are also expected to adhere to the clauses for the BNP networking workshops.

Endorsed meetings: BNP-ISBA supports and endorses other meetings, sessions, workshops to encourage activity in Bayesian nonparametrics. To request endorsement, please submit a single PDF file to bnp.isba.section@gmail.com, containing information on the event including Title, Dates, Location, Website (if available), Description, and BNP-ISBA representation (any involvement of BNP-ISBA members).
